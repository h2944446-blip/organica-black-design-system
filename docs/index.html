The Logo

The logo is an integral part of the brand and should be used thoughtfully, consistently, and in its entirety. Each version is shown on its approved background.

Full color — white backgrounds only
White — photos & colored backgrounds
Black — light colored backgrounds
Mark only — favicons, avatars, small spaces

Do

  • Keep clear space equal to the height of the tree mark
  • Scale proportionally
  • Pick the version with the strongest contrast

Don't

  • Rotate or distort the logo
  • Partially cover it with other elements
  • Display it directly on a photo
