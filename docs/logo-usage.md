# Logo Usage — Organica Black

The logo is an integral part of the brand and should be used thoughtfully and consistently. It is most often displayed in its full-color format and in its entirety.

## Versions

| Version | File | When to use |
|---|---|---|
| Full color (primary) | `../assets/logo/organica-logo-full-color.png` | White backgrounds **only** |
| White | `../assets/logo/organica-logo-white.png` | Photos and colored backgrounds |
| Black | `../assets/logo/organica-logo-black.png` | Light colored backgrounds |
| Mark only (purple / white / black) | `../assets/logo/organica-mark-*.png` | Favicons, app icons, avatars, watermarks, small spaces |

## Do

- Keep clear space around the logo (at minimum, the height of the tree mark on all sides)
- Scale proportionally
- Choose the version with the strongest contrast against the background

## Don't

- ❌ Rotate the logo
- ❌ Distort or stretch it
- ❌ Partially cover it with other elements
- ❌ Display it directly on a photo (use a solid backing or the white version on a darkened overlay)
- ❌ Recolor it outside the approved palette
